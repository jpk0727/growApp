Controller

This page has the ability to change what's going on in the growing environment. Please don't submit any changes unless you are authorized.

Humidity: The fan controlls the humidity, changing the value will adjust how the fan behaves
Watering: Changing the water values changes the amount of time each row is watered in seconds for during each watering cycle. Also, changing the frequency adjusts how often the watering takes place in seconds. Note, the effects of this adjustment only take place after the next watering event occurs.
Light ON/OFF: Change at which hour of the day the lights are turned on and off. Note, changing this takes at most 30 minutes to apply.
